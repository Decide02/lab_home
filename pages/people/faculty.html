--- layout: default title: Faculty permalink: /people/faculty/ --- # Faculty ## Biography I am an Assistant Professor in the [Department of Computer Science and Engineering](https://cse.pusan.ac.kr/cse/index.do){:target="_blank"} and the [College of BioMedical Convergence Engineering](https://bce.pusan.ac.kr/bce/index..do){:target="_blank"} at [Pusan National University](https://www.pusan.ac.kr/kor/Main.do){:target="_blank"}. Before joining PNU, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at UC Berkeley in the EECS Department. I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Yonsei University in 2022 under the guidance of Prof. Kwanghoon Sohn. I received my B.S. in the same department from Yonsei University in 2016. Additionally, I have worked as a research intern at Adobe and have been actively involved in various research collaborations.
